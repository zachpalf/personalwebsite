FAQ

Why will people come to your site? Users will come to my site because they are looking for a tax professional to help them file their taxes. Not only will I help them file their taxes, but I will help them save the most money they possibly can. Through these great benefits, many will seek the professional help they desire. This will allow clients to achieve their financial goals, have a successful business, and help them understand their finances. What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them? The questions visitors of my site will expect my site to answer for them are, what is the purpose of a tax accountant, what types of documents do I need to bring to the first meeting, and where can I find the office? My website will be able to address this question to visitors to my website will have. By addressing these questions, visitors will have more of a desire to become customers. What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site? Visitors of my website will, after learning about my company, want to find a time to visit with me to prepare having their taxes done. Due to this desire of visitors, I will provide information as to how they can contact me and when they can come into my office and meet with me. This will allow me to reach more customers. Thus, I will not have to rely on solely word of mouth to grow my company.

What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?

I will need to provide information about where my office is located, a phone number with which they can reach me at, and my business hours during the week. The providing of this information will allow visitors to get in contact with me either by walking into my office and set up an appointment or call and set up an appointment.

Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

Visitors will come to my website to obtain the information that I provide because I provide top of the line tax services. Visitors will want to see why the services I provide are better. Thus, my website will address why they should hire me as their tax accountant.
